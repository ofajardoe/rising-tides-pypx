Evidence in Colombia

Migration due to climate changes is a reality. In Colombia, regions like Cartagena, Barranquilla, Santa Marta, and Juanchaco are extreme hotspots for this crisis.

Climate Displacement Factsheet (2016-2022)

  • 536,000 internal displacements were recorded due to disasters.
  • 2022 Fact: Unprecedented record year accounting for 281,000 displacements (52.4% of total).
  • Economic Impact: At-risk regions contribute to 76% of our GDP. Projected loss of 1.5% to 2.5% by 2050 without action.

Call to Action
Bio-Economy

Bio Economy means to do money with sustainable actions helping the nature and the environment.

In Colombia, initiatives like the proposed "Climate Refugee" Bill (2023) aim to formally recognize and protect displaced individuals. Sustainable housing and resilient urban coastal planning are pivotal bio-economy actions.

Join us in spreading awareness and taking direct action to build a better future for displaced communities and our planet's oceans.
